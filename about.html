About Us

Who We Are

mini MoMA is a digital gallery designed to bring a curated selection of the Museum of Modern Art’s most iconic works to a wider audience. Created by an art enthusiast with a passion for both creativity and technology, this website offers a simple and inviting way to experience the highlights of modern and contemporary art history. We believe that great art should be easy to explore, no matter where you are.

Our Mission

At mini MoMA, our mission is to make the world of modern art more accessible, engaging, and inspiring. By highlighting key masterpieces and influential artists from the 1880s to today, we hope to spark curiosity, encourage new perspectives, and invite more people to appreciate the transformative power of visual art. Whether you’re discovering these works for the first time or deepening a lifelong passion, mini MoMA is here to make the journey welcoming and memorable.
